About this space

Cozy Studio in Balozi Road, Kitengela – Your Quiet Home Base on a Budget. Nestled on the peaceful Balozi Road, this warm and inviting studio offers an affordable escape without compromising on comfort. Designed with solo travelers, students, and budget-conscious professionals in mind, this space feels like a sanctuary away from the city's hustle. **Living Area:** Bright and welcoming, featuring a plush sofa and a 43-inch Smart TV – perfect for unwinding with your favourite shows after a long day. **Workspace:** A dedicated desk with an ergonomic chair and high-speed Wi-Fi (100 Mbps) makes remote work or study effortless. **Kitchenette:** Compact yet fully functional, equipped with a mini-fridge, microwave, coffee maker, and basic cookware – everything you need to prepare light meals and your morning brew. **Sleeping Area:** A queen-size memory foam mattress, blackout curtains, and premium cotton linens promise restful nights and refreshed mornings. Enjoy the tranquillity of Balozi Road – a quiet residential area that feels removed from the noise yet remains just 3 minutes from Kitengela town. Supermarkets (Naivas, Quickmart), hospitals (Halisi Family Hospital), and local eateries are all within easy reach. The Nairobi-Namanga Road is minutes away, making commuting to Nairobi or JKIA straightforward. Whether you're passing through, studying, or working remotely, this studio offers exceptional value in a peaceful setting. Call or WhatsApp 0723562484 to book your stay.
