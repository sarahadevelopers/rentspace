Security & Transport Reality in Kitengela

Security: All properties we list are inside gated estates with 24/7 guards, CCTV, and controlled access. Kitengela has a lower crime rate than many Nairobi suburbs like Embakasi or Kayole.

Transport to Nairobi: The commute takes 40-60 minutes off-peak (via Mombasa Road). During peak hours (6:30–8:30 AM, 5–7 PM), expect 90 minutes. Use the Kitengela-Nairobi commuter train from Athi River station – only KES 150 and avoids traffic.

Getting around Kitengela: Boda bodas (KES 50-100 per trip) and matatus (KES 30-50) are abundant. Uber/Bolt also operate but can be scarce late at night.
