Living in Kitengela

Cost of Living: Kitengela offers affordable living compared to Nairobi CBD. Basic utilities (water, electricity) range from KES 3,000–8,000 per month.

Schools: Acacia Crest Academy, Kitengela International School, Milimani Academy.

Hospitals: Halisi Family Hospital, Kitengela Nursing Home.

Shopping Centres: Kitengela Mall, Signature Mall, Carrefour.

Transport: Easy access to Namanga Road with 24/7 matatus to Nairobi CBD, JKIA, and the SGR station.

Security: Most estates have gated communities with electric fences, 24/7 guards, and CCTV.

Best Estates: Milimani, Chuna, Acacia, Yukos.

Average Rents: 2‑bedroom apartments from KES 23,000; 3‑bedroom bungalows from KES 45,000; luxury homes up to KES 150,000.

Frequently Asked Questions About Kitengela

Is Kitengela safe?

Yes, Kitengela has many gated communities with 24/7 security, electric fences, and active neighbourhood watches.

How much is rent in Kitengela?

Rent ranges from KES 23,000/month for a 2‑bedroom apartment to over KES 100,000 for luxury homes.

How far is Kitengela from Nairobi CBD?

Kitengela is about 45‑60 minutes from Nairobi CBD via Namanga Road.

Which estates are popular in Kitengela?

Popular estates include Milimani, Chuna, Acacia, and Yukos.
