Spacious 3-Bedroom Duplex to Let in Syokimau – KES 35,000/month. Located in a quiet, gated court just off Mombasa Road, this well-designed duplex offers the perfect balance of privacy and convenience for families or professionals. Upstairs, the master bedroom comes with an en-suite bathroom and built-in wardrobes, while two additional bedrooms share a second bathroom.
