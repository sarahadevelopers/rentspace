Executive 5-Bedroom All-Ensuite Mansion with 2 Bonus Bedsitters – Kitengela, Muigai Area (KSh 70,000). Designed for expansive family living, this modern home features light-filled living and dining areas, a contemporary kitchen, and 5 ensuite bedrooms. A standout feature is the two self-contained bedsitters at the rear, perfect for staff quarters, a guest wing, or generating rental income (ideal for homeowners who want extra passive income).
